Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how [Mark Edrian Talavera] ("we", "us") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use the Orient1 Android application (the "App"). The App is an educational tool designed for campus orientation and learning activities, which may include lessons, a quiz module, augmented reality (AR) scenes, and a campus map with optional GPS-based navigation.

Quick Summary
No account required No advertising Camera used for AR only Location used for navigation (optional) Data stays on device unless you choose to share

Information We Collect

We designed the App to work primarily offline. We do not collect personal information unless you provide it or enable features that require it.

  • Camera Data (Required for AR): The App accesses your device camera to render AR content. Camera input is processed in real time on your device and is not stored or transmitted by us.
  • Location Data (Optional): If you enable location permissions, the App may access approximate or precise location to show your position on the campus map and provide navigation. Location data is used in-session and not stored or sent to our servers.
  • Quiz/Progress Data: The app does not store your quiz answers. They are only displayed once you complete a quiz.

Permissions We Request

Permission Why We Need It Is It Required?
CAMERA To enable augmented reality (AR) scenes and overlays. Yes (for AR features)
ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION To show your position on the campus map and provide optional GPS-based navigation. No (only if you use navigation features)
INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE For optional updates, loading remote resources if enabled, or sending support emails/logs that you initiate. No (core offline features work without it)
WAKE_LOCK To prevent the screen from sleeping during active AR sessions. No (quality-of-life optimization)
VIBRATE To provide haptic feedback in interactions (if implemented). No

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate App features (lessons, quizzes, AR scenes, and navigation).
  • Improve stability and performance based on anonymized diagnostics you choose to share.
  • Respond to your support requests.

Data Storage & Retention

  • On-Device: The app does not permanently store quiz answers or progress. Information is only shown temporarily during use and is cleared once you exit or complete the activity.
  • Server Storage: We do not maintain cloud servers for this App. If you email us logs or feedback, those messages are stored by our email provider according to their policies.

Third-Party Services

The App may include third‑party SDKs strictly to enable AR features (e.g., ARCore) or standard Android components. We do not serve ads and do not use analytics that track you across apps.

  • AR SDKs (e.g., Google ARCore): Used to process device sensors and camera frames for AR. See the respective provider’s privacy policy for details.

Your Choices & Controls

  • You can deny or revoke permissions at any time in Settings → Apps → Orient1 → Permissions. Some features may not function without the relevant permission.
  • You can delete local data by clearing the App’s storage or uninstalling the App.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information processed by the App. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we aim to minimize collection and keep data on-device whenever possible.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information you provide to us. Because we do not maintain cloud accounts or profiles, most actions can be performed directly on your device (e.g., uninstall to delete local data). You can also contact us for assistance.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect operational or legal changes. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date above. Your continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the revised policy.
